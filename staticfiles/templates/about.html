About Sri Krishnanugraha Idol Shop


At Sri Krishnanugraha, we believe in bringing the sacred and the beautiful into everyday life. Our shop is dedicated to offering a wide range of meticulously crafted idols, timeless antiques, and essential pooja samagri,various types of malas, deepas,etc to enhance your spiritual journey and home decor.

Started about 45 years ago by Manjunatha N M, our mission is to provide high-quality, authentic items that resonate with your faith and traditions. Each product in our collection is carefully selected for its craftsmanship, cultural significance, and spiritual value.

Whether you are looking for a stunning statue of your favorite deity, a unique antique piece to add character to your home, or the essential items for your daily rituals and festive celebrations, Divine Treasures is your one-stop destination. Our products are sourced from skilled artisans and trusted suppliers, ensuring that you receive only the best.

We are passionate about preserving and promoting our rich heritage and spirituality. We hope our collection inspires you and brings peace, prosperity, and joy into your life.

Thank you for choosing Sri Krishnanugraha. We look forward to serving you and being a part of your spiritual and cultural journey.
